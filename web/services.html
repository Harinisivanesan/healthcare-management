CT SCANS

A CT scan (computed tomography) scan is a non-invasive procedure that alloes providers to view 3D images take from a series of 2D X-Ray images.A CT scan is a diagnostic imaging procedure that uses a combination of X-rays and computer technology to produce images of the inside the body.It shows detailed images of any part of the body including the bones,muscles,fat,organs and blood vessels.
